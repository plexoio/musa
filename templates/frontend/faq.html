A VoteCard represents an event or topic on which users can vote. Each VoteCard may have multiple candidates or choices.
Navigate to the desired VoteCard's page and select the candidate or option you want to vote for. Then, simply click on the "Vote" button. Remember, you must be logged in and verified to vote.

"Draft" signifies the VoteCard is being prepared and is not yet visible to users. "Online" means the VoteCard is active and users can vote on it. "Cancelled" indicates the voting event has been rejected or terminated prematurely, and no further voting is possible. "Completed" signifies the voting period has ended and results are finalized.
Navigate to the VoteCard's page or to the 'Completed' entry in the 'Event' dropdown menu to view the results. The candidate with the most votes is declared the winner.
In the event of a tie, where two or more candidates receive the same number of votes, the system will recognize the result as a draw. Further action or resolution methods (e.g., a runoff vote) might be decided upon by the event organizers or administrators.
